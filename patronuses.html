Harry Potter – Stag 🦌

Harry’s Patronus is a stag, a symbol of bravery and leadership. It represents his father’s Animagus form, showing the deep bond between them. The stag also reflects Harry’s role as a protector of his friends.

Hermione Granger – Otter 🦦

Hermione’s Patronus is an otter, playful and intelligent. This animal shows her curiosity and clever mind. It also highlights her softer, fun side, which she doesn’t always show.

Ron Weasley – Jack Russell Terrier 🐕

Ron’s Patronus is a Jack Russell Terrier, known for loyalty and courage. Terriers love chasing things, just like Ron’s determination. It also connects to Hermione’s otter, showing their close relationship.

Severus Snape – Doe 🦌

Snape’s Patronus is a doe, the same as Lily Potter’s. This represents his everlasting love and devotion to her. It also shows his hidden, softer side beneath his harsh exterior.

Lily Potter – Doe 🦌

Lily’s Patronus is a doe, gentle and kind. It reflects her caring nature and the love she gave to those around her. Her Patronus matches her compassion and protective spirit.

Albus Dumbledore – Phoenix 🔥🦅

Dumbledore’s Patronus is a phoenix, symbolizing rebirth and hope. It connects to his companion Fawkes. The phoenix represents his wisdom and his ability to bring light in dark times.

Kingsley Shacklebolt – Lynx 🐆

Kingsley’s Patronus is a lynx, strong and protective. It reflects his powerful presence and skill as a wizard. He even used it to send important messages during the war.

Luna Lovegood – Hare 🐇

Luna’s Patronus is a hare, quick and unique. It shows her individuality and creativity. Like Luna, the hare is gentle but fast, always surprising those who underestimate it.

Ginny Weasley – Horse 🐎

Ginny’s Patronus is a horse, symbolizing strength and freedom. It represents her independence and bold character. Horses also show energy and courage, just like Ginny’s personality.

Cho Chang – Swan 🦢

Cho’s Patronus is a swan, elegant and graceful. It represents beauty and loyalty. However, the swan can also symbolize sadness, matching her struggles with loss and love.
