The Houses

🦁 Gryffindor

Traits: Brave, courageous, determined. Definition: Gryffindor is the house for those who value bravery and chivalry. Its members are bold, daring, and often natural leaders.

🐍 Slytherin

Traits: Ambitious, cunning, resourceful. Definition: Slytherin is for those who seek power and achievement. Members are determined, strategic, and know how to use opportunities to succeed.

🦅 Ravenclaw

Traits: Intelligent, wise, creative. Definition: Ravenclaw values learning, knowledge, and wit. Its members are curious, original thinkers who love discovering new ideas.

🦡 Hufflepuff

Traits: Loyal, kind, hardworking. Definition: Hufflepuff is known for fairness, patience, and dedication. Members of this house are friendly, just, and value teamwork.
